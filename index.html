About Us

Kartavya is an NGO run by students of the Indian Institute of Technology (Indian School of Mines), Dhanbad. Established with the aim of bringing about positive social change, Kartavya is actively involved in various philanthropic activities and community development initiatives.

One of the key initiatives of Kartavya is its education program. The NGO volunteers organize regular tutoring sessions and mentorship programs for students from economically disadvantaged backgrounds. They aim to bridge the educational gap by providing academic support, career guidance, and skills development opportunities.
